THE ERLANG AND ELIXIR CONFERENCE
NYC | November 15, 2024

One day celebration of all things BEAM is coming to New York!

This event offers a unique opportunity to meet locally, learn from industry experts, and network with fellow Erlang and Elixir enthusiasts.

Early Bird sale opens on Sept 12 and will be live until it sells out or until Oct 2!

Share. Learn. Inspire.

The Conference for Erlang and Elixir Developers in NYC

We strive to provide a meaningful platform for local Erlang and Elixir communities, and that's why we're organizing Code BEAM Lite in NYC.

Join us to explore the latest advancements in the BEAM ecosystem, including groundbreaking talks on AI & Machine Learning, Fintech, IoT, Security, DevOps and innovative use cases.

This event offers a unique opportunity to meet locally, learn from industry experts, and network with fellow Erlang and Elixir enthusiasts.

Would you like to participate as a sponsor of this event? We need support from the community to make Code BEAM Lite NYC the best event it can possibly be. In return, we offer sponsors the opportunity to showcase technology and hire new talent. Explore our sponsorship possibilities!
